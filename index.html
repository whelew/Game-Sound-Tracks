The Most Iconic Video Game Soundtracks

Welcome to Video Game Sound Tracks. Where we look at and go through some of the most iconic, and well known video game soundtracks out there. We will look at what chords are being used, the instrumentation and why it works so well for the game it was designed for. If you are a composer or just a fan of the music you've come to the right place!

Chords:
Chords define the harmony of the track. They are powerful tools at invoking certain types of emotion needed for game soundtracks.

Instrumentation:
These are the instruments, tones and sounds used in the track. The aesthetic of the game often justifies the choice in instruments.

Genre:
The Genre of a game often defines the style of soundtrack it will have. Fantasy games often have big orchestral scores for example.
